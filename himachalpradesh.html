Himachal Pradesh

The traditional dresses of Himachal Pradesh are different for different tribes and vary among both men and women as well.the dresses are made keeping in mind the harsh cold weather during winters that linger almost throughout the year. The women usually wear salwar kameez, Ghagras, and Lehenga Cholis. The costumes of women include colorful headscarves that are long enough and are knotted from the back. Women also adorn themselves with lots of bangles, and different types of traditional ornaments.
